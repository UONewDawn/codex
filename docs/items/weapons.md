# Weapons

Guide to weapons and their properties in New Dawn.

## Weapon Types

### Swords

**Swordsmanship Skill Required**

- **Longsword:** Balanced damage and speed
- **Broadsword:** Moderate damage
- **Katana:** Fast, lower damage
- **Viking Sword:** Good all-around
- **Halberd:** Two-handed, high damage

**Damage Type:** Physical/Slashing

---

### Fencing Weapons

**Fencing Skill Required**

- **Kryss:** Fast, popular
- **Dagger:** Very fast, low damage
- **War Fork:** Balanced
- **Spear:** Two-handed reach

**Damage Type:** Physical/Piercing

---

### Maces

**Mace Fighting Skill Required**

- **Mace:** Standard crushing
- **War Mace:** Heavy hitter
- **Maul:** Slow, powerful
- **War Hammer:** Good balance
- **Club:** Basic weapon

**Damage Type:** Physical/Bashing
**Bonus:** Extra damage vs. undead

---

### Archery

**Archery Skill Required**

- **Bow:** Standard ranged
- **Crossbow:** Slow, high damage
- **Heavy Crossbow:** Highest damage

**Ammunition:**
- Arrows (bows)
- Bolts (crossbows)

**Damage Type:** Physical/Ranged

## Weapon Stats

### Damage

Base damage range:
- Example: 15-30 damage
- Modified by STR
- Enhanced by tactics

### Speed

How fast weapon swings:
- Fast: Katana, Kryss
- Medium: Most swords
- Slow: Two-handed weapons

### Requirements

Strength needed to wield:
- Light: 10-30 STR
- Medium: 40-60 STR
- Heavy: 70-100 STR

## Special Moves

Many weapons have special abilities:

- **Armor Ignore:** Bypass all armor
- **Disarm:** Remove opponent's weapon
- **Double Strike:** Hit twice quickly
- **Concussion:** Drain mana
- **Paralyzing Blow:** Freeze target

## Weapon Materials

### Metals

- **Iron:** Standard
- **Dull Copper:** +10% Durability
- **Shadow Iron:** Physical resist
- **Copper:** Energy resist
- **Bronze:** Poison resist
- **Gold:** Luck bonus
- **Agapite:** Cold resist
- **Verite:** Fire resist
- **Valorite:** Multiple bonuses

## Choosing a Weapon

Consider:

1. **Your Skill:** Match weapon to skill
2. **Speed vs. Damage:** Personal preference
3. **Special Move:** What ability helps you
4. **Requirements:** Can you use it?
5. **Situation:** PvP vs. PvM

!!! tip "Weapon Selection"
    - Try different weapons
    - Match to your playstyle
    - Keep a variety in bank
    - Specialize once you find favorites
