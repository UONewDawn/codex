# Armor

Protect yourself with various armor types in New Dawn.

## Armor Types

### Plate Armor

**Highest Protection**

**Pieces:**
- Plate Helm
- Plate Gorget
- Plate Chest
- Plate Arms
- Plate Gloves
- Plate Legs

**Pros:**
- Maximum physical protection
- High durability
- Prestigious appearance

**Cons:**
- Heavy (movement penalty)
- Dexterity loss
- No meditation (blocks mana regen)
- Expensive

**Best For:** Melee warriors, tanks

---

### Chainmail Armor

**Medium Protection**

**Pieces:**
- Chain Coif
- Chain Tunic
- Chain Leggings

**Pros:**
- Good protection
- Moderate weight
- Less dex penalty than plate
- Affordable

**Cons:**
- Can't meditate
- Less protection than plate

**Best For:** Warriors on budget, mid-level fighters

---

### Leather Armor

**Light Protection**

**Pieces:**
- Leather Cap
- Leather Gorget
- Leather Tunic
- Leather Arms
- Leather Gloves
- Leather Legs

**Pros:**
- Allows meditation
- Light weight
- No dex penalty
- Cheap

**Cons:**
- Low physical protection
- Lower durability

**Best For:** Mages, archers, stealthy characters

---

### Studded Leather

**Improved Leather**

Same pieces as leather but:

**Pros:**
- Better protection than leather
- Still allows meditation (usually)
- Moderate weight
- Good for rangers

**Cons:**
- More expensive than leather
- Check server rules on meditation

**Best For:** Rangers, dexterity builds, hybrid chars

## Armor Stats

### Armor Rating (AR)

Total protection value:
- Plate: 40-50 AR
- Chain: 28-35 AR
- Studded: 24-30 AR
- Leather: 13-18 AR

### Durability

How much damage armor can take:
- Repairs reduce max durability
- High-quality lasts longer
- Exceptional items more durable

### Weight

Affects movement and stamina:
- Plate: Very heavy
- Chain: Moderate
- Studded: Light-medium
- Leather: Light

## Armor Materials

### Leather Types

- **Regular:** Standard protection
- **Spined:** Physical resist
- **Horned:** Fire resist
- **Barbed:** Poison resist

### Metal Types

Same as weapons:
- Iron through Valorite
- Each provides different bonuses

## Magical Armor

Properties commonly found:

- **+AR:** Additional protection
- **+Resistances:** Elemental protection
- **+Stats:** STR/DEX/INT bonuses
- **+Skills:** Skill bonuses
- **Luck:** Better loot
- **Mage Armor:** Meditation in metal

## Shields

Additional defense:

### Shield Types

- **Buckler:** Light, small bonus
- **Bronze Shield:** Medium
- **Heater Shield:** Good protection
- **Metal Shield:** Best AR
- **Wooden Shield:** Lightweight

**Requires:** Parrying skill for full benefit

## Choosing Armor

Consider:

1. **Your Build:** Mage vs. Warrior
2. **Meditation:** Need mana regen?
3. **Protection:** How much do you need?
4. **Budget:** What can you afford?
5. **Resistances:** What damage type?

!!! tip "Armor Tips"
    - Mages: Stick to leather/studded
    - Warriors: Plate for maximum defense
    - Archers: Studded for balance
    - Mix materials for resistance coverage
