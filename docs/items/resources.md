# Resources

Essential materials and resources for crafting and survival.

## Crafting Resources

### Metals & Ores

**Iron**
- Most common ore
- Base for all metal items
- Found everywhere

**Colored Ores** (if available)
- Dull Copper
- Shadow Iron
- Copper
- Bronze
- Gold
- Agapite
- Verite
- Valorite

**Gathering:** Mining skill + pickaxe

---

### Wood & Lumber

**Regular Logs**
- Common wood
- All basic carpentry
- Found at any tree

**Colored Woods** (if available)
- Oak
- Ash
- Yew
- Heartwood
- Bloodwood
- Frostwood

**Gathering:** Lumberjacking + axe

---

### Leather & Hides

**Regular Leather**
- From most animals
- Basic tailoring
- Common resource

**Colored Leather**
- Spined (medium creatures)
- Horned (tough creatures)
- Barbed (powerful creatures)

**Gathering:** Kill animals, use blade on corpse

---

### Cloth & Fabric

**Cotton/Cloth**
- Purchase from vendors
- Craft into items
- Make bandages

**Thread**
- Made from cotton
- Tailoring requirement

---

### Reagents

**Magic Components:**
- Black Pearl
- Blood Moss
- Garlic
- Ginseng
- Mandrake Root
- Nightshade
- Spider's Silk
- Sulfurous Ash

**Source:** Mage shops, herb gathering

---

### Gems

**Common Gems:**
- Amber
- Amethyst
- Citrine
- Ruby
- Emerald
- Sapphire
- Diamond

**Uses:**
- Jewelry crafting
- Decoration
- Value storage

**Source:** Mining

## Consumable Resources

### Food

Keep fed for stat regeneration:
- Bread, cheese, meat
- Fish
- Cooked meals
- Beverages

### Potions

**Healing Potions:**
- Lesser Heal
- Heal
- Greater Heal

**Other Potions:**
- Cure (remove poison)
- Refresh (restore stamina)
- Explosion (damage)
- Various stat potions

### Bandages

Essential healing supply:
- Made from cloth (tailoring)
- Stack of 50-100 recommended
- Required for Healing skill

## Tool Resources

### Crafting Tools

- **Pickaxe/Shovel:** Mining
- **Axe/Hatchet:** Lumberjacking
- **Tinker Tools:** Tinkering
- **Sewing Kit:** Tailoring
- **Smith Hammer:** Blacksmithy
- **Saw:** Carpentry
- **Mortar & Pestle:** Alchemy
- **Scribe's Pen:** Inscription

**Durability:** Tools wear out with use

## Resource Gathering

### Best Locations

**Mining:**
- Minoc mountains
- Vesper mountains
- Mountain caves

**Lumberjacking:**
- Yew forest
- Any dense forest
- Spiritwood areas

**Hunting (leather):**
- Forest animals
- Swamp creatures
- Special spawns for rare hides

### Gathering Tips

!!! success "Efficient Gathering"
    - Bring pack animal (beetle, llama)
    - Mark runes to good spots
    - Gather in bulk
    - Watch for resource respawns
    - Join resource gathering guilds

## Resource Economy

### Selling Resources

- Sell to crafters
- Set up vendor
- Bulk sales get better prices

### Buying Resources

- Player vendors (variable prices)
- NPC shops (expensive, limited)
- Other players (negotiate)

---

**Gather resources, fuel the economy!**
