# Special Items

Unique, rare, and special items in New Dawn.

## Unique Items

### Runes & Runebooks

**Recall Runes:**
- Mark locations
- Recall to them
- Essential for travel

**Runebooks:**
- Store multiple runes (16)
- Organize travel spots
- Charges for recalls

**House Keys:**

**Container Keys:**
- Lock chests
- Secure valuables

- Valuable items

**Commodity Deeds:**

- Crafting efficiency
