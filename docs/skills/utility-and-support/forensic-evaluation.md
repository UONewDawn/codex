# Forensic Evaluation

## Overview

Forensic Evaluation lets you inspect corpses, players, and containers to uncover who killed, looted, or tampered with them.

## How it works

Using the skill:

- On a player, reveals if they belong to the Thieves Guild.
- On a corpse, reveals who killed it and who looted it.
- On a lockable chest, reveals who recently picked the lock.

## Training

Train from Healer NPCs to reach around 50.

Repeatedly use the skill on corpses until reaching 100.
