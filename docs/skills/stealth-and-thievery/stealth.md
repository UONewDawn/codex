# Stealth

## Overview

Stealth allows you to move while hidden, staying invisible to others. Essential complement to Hiding skill, enabling mobile stealth gameplay for scouts, thieves, and assassins.

## How it works

You will need at least 80 Hiding or you won't be able to use Stealth.

First you need to be hidden, then use Stealth, is successful it will allow you to take a certain amount of steps while remaining invisible, running or failing will reveal you.

So for example you could Hide, use Stealth, take some steps, use Stealth again to reset the steps and keep moving.

Stepping on someone else will also reveal you.

You can't use Stealth while riding a mount.

The higher the armor rating you wear, the harder it is to stealth.

At GM Stealth you can move hidden with an AR of 15.

When stealthing around others there is a chance of being detected. A check is made by comparing your Stealth and DEX against the Detecting Hidden and INT of players around you.

## Training

Train from Thief NPCs to reach around 50.

Stealth can be used on a 10 seconds cooldown, wear the right amount of armor rating and keep using Stealth while hidden until you reach 100.

| Skill    | Wear AR |
|----------|---------|
| up to 60 | up to 5 |
| 60 - 65  | 6       |
| 65 - 70  | 7       |
| 70 - 73  | 8       |
| 73 - 75  | 9       |
| 75 - 78  | 11      |
| 78 - 82  | 12      |
| 82 - 90  | 13      |
| 90 - 100 | 14      |
