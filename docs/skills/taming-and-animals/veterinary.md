# Veterinary

## Overview

Veterinary is the pet equivalent of Healing. It allows you to heal and resurrect pets and animals using bandages. Absolutely essential for tamers and useful for anyone with pets.

Also helps increasing your stable slots.

At 60 you will be able to cure poison and at 80 to resurrect pets.

Starting items if you choose this skill in character creation: Scissors, Bandages.

## Training

Train from Animal trainer NPCs to reach around 50.

Have two horses fight each other while healing them with bandages until reaching 100.
