# SERVER PATCH 1.6

## March 3rd, 2026

This patch introduces new house types, the launch of the daily rare system, dueling pits in Dawn, and new horse breeds to discover and trade. Along with that, bug fixes and system tweaks continue based on feedback and observations.

### 🛠️ Bug Fixes & Adjustments

- Your spirit speak skill now effects your summon casting chance on 8th circle spells. At GM spirit speak, and spirit speak is enabled, you have a 80% chance to be successful
- Fertile dirt is now required for all plants. The drop rate for fertile dirt has been increased a bunch
- Fixed single-click label on seeds
- Fixed fire breath calc on the Gargoyle Warlord in the Codex Rift
- Added worn labels to pick axes
- Fixed the tailoring dye kit to correctly accept the amount in the stack
- If you're at the house limit, you can now still see the "ghost" version of the house using the tool
- Big fish now display the time in local server time (central US)
- Fixed an issue with Barracoon
- Made several adjustments to the chances when digging up artifacts, also expanded clues to cover other ruined towns
- Adjusted how recall works on runebooks. You still don't have a magery requirement when using charges, but you still go through the casting motion
- Fixed LOS issue accessing chests and runebooks through walls
- Player vendor cost per real world day has been reduced
- All new achievements for crafters, tmaps, MIBs, Rift Masters, and a few others things (from 839 to over 2600 achievements now available)
- The 650 point tailoring BOD reward slot has been populated with unique and random tailoring deco (6 unique items)

!!! tip "Dev Note"
    The initial plan pre-launch was to have power scrolls for tailoring, and when you'd hit 120, you could craft hoods. Before launch, I worried the market would be flooded with hoods, so I removed them with the intention of going back to fill the gap, which I didn't do until now. Apologies to those who turned in a 650 and reported getting nothing, you'll be able to get something.

- When an IDOC is announced, the house sign will also reflect if that house is IDOC.

!!! tip "Dev Note"
    Just a reminder, when a house is announced as IDOC, it's about a 2 hour window before it falls. Not your traditional 14 hour window.

### 📅 "Daily" Rares

The daily rare system has been activated, with rares now found in **all new locations** from those found in beta.

- **What are daily rares?** They're deco items that spawn randomly throughout the world for players to find, collect, sell, and/or display.
- **What does "activated" mean?** Each rare item spawns between a range of days. For some, it might be 3-5 days, for others could be 20-25 days. They aren't on a static interval.
- **Where are the rares?** With the system active, it will start spawning rares into the world when those timers are triggered. In other words, the world isn't full of all the rare items that can be found post-patch, they will spawn over time.
