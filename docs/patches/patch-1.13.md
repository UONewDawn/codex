# SERVER PATCH 1.13

## June 28th, 2026

Alongside with bug fixes and quality-of-life improvements, this update introducing bee keeping, and some pirate map changes.

## 🛠️ Bug Fixes & Enhancements

- Fixed issue with special elementals not spawning while mining with a gargoyle pickaxe
- Common honey can now be purchased again from the vendors, and cooking recipes that use honey should work again
- Additional fixes for pet AI and movement issues
- Potential fix for pets moving slow when not in combat
- Fixed breakfast tray art item when flipped
- Added new creature entries across multiple Slayer Groups (humanoid, undead, fey, elemental, arachnid, abyss, reptilian) so that they're both vulnerable to the matching slayer weapons and additional creatures have a chance to drop a slayer
- Small carpentry BODs will have a chance at requiring non-regular wood
- Fixed several achievements that were sharing duplicate titles with other achievements
- Fixed issue with an attacking pet not going gray if they attack a blue pet
- Added several missing hunting achievements
- New auctions get announced in #trade
- The rift reward sandals are now blessed, and have a special label. If you want to swap your existing ones, use #submit-a-ticket

!!! note
    If you'd like to swap your existing ones, visit #submit-a-ticket

## ⚓ Ship Holds - Home Below Deck

I'm excited to roll out the first version of something I've wanted in UO since I first signed up for the beta back in the 90's -- the ability to go in your ship's hold, store stuff down there, and set up a "home away from home."

To learn more, check out the [Ship Holds](../custom-systems/ship-holds.md) page.


## 🐝 Beekeeping

- Fixed potion icons to align with the correct type on the beehive gump
- After you place a beehive, it will be in a Dormant mode, and you can chop it to get the deed back to move it
- Double-clicking the hive activates it, and you can no longer chop it down
- You can move an active hive, by standing in the spot you want to move it to in your house. This does increase by 4 hours the next check time, since moving a beehive is very disruptive to bees
- If you want to destroy a hive, you need to use the button in the gump, unless it's empty/dead
- The hive check is now a true 24 hours
- Honey not identified after 3 attempts will be identified as wild flower or clover
- Slight adjustment to the min/max values of some honey orders

## 🏠 New House Styles

Three new houses are available:

- A brown keep, facing south, with a northeast courtyard with 2 recall spots, fencing, and 'secret' door
- A gray keep, facing east, with a courtyard in the southwest with 1 recall spot, and a 'secret' door
- A three story small tower, this one facing east
