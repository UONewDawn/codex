# SERVER PATCH 1.13

## Xth, 2026

Alongside with bug fixes and quality-of-life improvements, this update introducing bee keeping, and some pirate map changes.

## 🛠️ Bug Fixes & Enhancements

- Fixed issue with special elementals not spawning while mining with a gargoyle pickaxe
- Common honey can now be purchased again from the vendors, and cooking recipes use it
- Additional fixes for pet AI and movement issues
- Potential fix for pets moving slow when not in combat
- Fixed breakfast tray art item when flipped
- Added new creature entries across multiple Slayer Groups, including humanoid, undead, fey, elemental, arachnid, abyss, and reptilian groups that both are affcted by, and drop slayers
- Small carpentry BODs will have a chance at requiring non-regular word
- Fixed several achievements that had the titles that other achievements also had
- Fixed issue with an attacking pet not going gray if they attack a blue pet
- New auctions get announced in #trade

### 🐝 Beekeeping

- Fixed potion icons on the beehive gump
- After you place a beehive, you can chop it to get the deed back to move it. After you double-click the hive, that activates it
- You can move an active hive, by standing in the spot you want to move it to in your house. This does increase by 4 hours the next check time, since moving a beehive is very disruptive to bees
- You can no longer directly chop a hive, you must use the destroy button in the gump, unless it's empty/dead
- The hive check is now a true 24 hours
- Honey not identified after 3 attempts will be identified as wild flower or clover
- Slight adjustment to the min/max values of some honey orders

## ⚓ Ship Holds - Home Below Deck

I'm excited to roll out the first version of something I've wanted in UO since I first signed up for the beta back in the 90's -- the ability to go in your ship's hold, store stuff down there, and setup a "home away from home."

To learn more, check out the [Ship Holds](../custom-systems/ship-holds.md) page.
