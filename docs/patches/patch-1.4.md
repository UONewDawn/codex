## February 22nd, 2026

## üåÄ Codex Rifts

Codex Rifts have seen a slight overhaul with the goal of making the fights feel a bit more challenging and scaling up in difficulty as you go through the tiers of mobs. The loot pools have also been adjusted on the tier level mobs.

Rift Masters are a bit more difficult now, with special abilities. Also, all Rift Masters have had their HP pools increased, and can also drop up to 5 to 8 shards, depending on the rift master. They also now have a small circle of gold that drops, similar to champs.
