# SERVER PATCH 1.9

## April 6th, 2026

This patch continues to make some slight adjustments overall, and introduces some new houses, CTF, and pirate regions.

## 🛠️ Bug Fixes & Adjustments

- Significancy lowered the chances peculiar seeds that drop off mobs to try to balance out the amount of plants being grown
- Large BODs will now show a picture of the item that needs to be crafted to avoid any confusion
- Fixed the countdown on the explosion timer
- Public moongate gump now lets you go to Dawn
- Mage based mobs have a cooldown on how often they can attempt to dispel
- Rare horses, once tamed, can be transferred to any player regardless of skill level
- Slight adjustment to how carpentry BODs points are calculated (5 items used to be worth 400 points, now its 350. 6 items are now worth 400). This also fixes large six item BODs that were reporting less than 100 points
- Resource Gather/Harvesting achievements should be available again
- Fixed issue in dueling pits allowing you create additional explosion pots
- The text color on material in BOD Books is now readable
- Shoes and lanterns will display the hue number if it's special
- Adjusted the Z tolerance when using bandages on your pets

## 🐰 Happy Easter!

When you log in, you will have an Easter Day bag placed in your bank that will have various random rolls and chances at easter themed clothing and deco. Claim yours by April 9th!

Enjoy and thanks for playing on New Dawn!

The Governors of Housing have ruled that henceforth, structures crafted from sandstone -- the very material drawn from the deserts and shores of Britannia -- may be placed upon the sands from which they came. It is only fitting that a home built of the land should be permitted to stand upon it.

## 🚩Capture the Flag

You can now play Capture the Flag any time and earn war tokens that can be used to purchase unique rewards from the CTF vendor in Dawn!

Want to play? Go to Dawn, find the CTF signup book, and join up! CTF is always available

The Order of the Silver Serpent, charged with the security of these southern seas, issues the following warning to all who sail near the islands known as Bald Island and Amoeba Island.

Both islands have fallen to pirate occupation. Despite our vigilance, the lawless nature of these shores has rendered them beyond the reach of order and justice.
