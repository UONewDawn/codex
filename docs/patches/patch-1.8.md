# SERVER PATCH 1.8

## March 23rd, 2026

This is a smaller patch that continues to address bugs and feedback to the game. Development on enhancements to pirate maps, Order vs Chaos, CTF, and other systems will continue.

## 🛠️ Bug Fixes & Adjustments

- Fixed issue with pet's stay behavior
- Fixed issue with carpentry BODs not being handed out if you didn't have the require skills
- Added a few more items to the carpentry BOD list
- Instruments, shields, or staves added to a BOD must have a maker's mark
- You can turn on "Frozen While Casting" in `[profile` which will prevent you from moving at all when casting a spell (vs. the spamming rubberband effect)
- Fixed issue with ethereal hues not applying when in your bag
- Fixed issue with tamed or summoned pets attacking and causing you to go gray
- Fixed additional issues with how the considering your sins gump displays remaining time
- Fixed the issue when you go from 5 short terms to 4 short term kills, it applied the 24 hour, not 16 hour decay
- If you're in a guild, you can opt-in (you're opt'ed out by default) using `[profile` to having your position shared with the rest of your guild on the World Map
- Adjustments to the large brick house and two story houses (price, lockdowns)
- The 2 pirate maps per 24 hours is now based on Central US time (not UTC)
- You can see hidden players in your party
- Potential fix to forever reactive armor
- Daily rare system timer has been fixed
- With 100 tracking, you can now track sea creatures
- Fixed issue with shafts not creating the correct amount. 1 board = 6 shafts, and you can process max 100 boards at a time.
- Added new rewards to the Rift store
- Fixed issue with MIBs and ship's strong boxes
- Moved the Catch & Release release feature to `[profile` instead of being a per-fishing pole setting. If enabled, fish will be thrown back when caught (fish only)
- A new explosion potion will be placed in your bag when dueling
- The boat IDOC message will be a smaller window (it was assuming 5 days was decay rate, not 9)
- Removed the Medium Dueling Tower for the time being
- The "mightly gorilla" is no more :(
- Detect hidden in a house was slightly changed to simply reveal everyone in the house, instead of boosting your skill to 120 and giving you 22 tiles range

## Happy St. Patrick's Day

Sorry, better late than never! When you log in, you will have a St. Patrick's 2026 reward bag in your Bank.

Among the items everyone will get, here is a quick rundown of the random rolls on your bag:

- Random chance at a limited dye tub hue with one of two St. Patrick's Day hues
- Everyone will get a pair of sandals, but depending on your roll, they could be part of the rarer hued variety
- As a nod to my favorite beer, barreled aged stouts will be handed out, with some random rare variants (just like when I used to stand out at 3am for the stuff)
- A few Irish inspired food items, with rarer rolls being the tray version of them
