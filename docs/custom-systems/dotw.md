# Dungeon of the Week

Every Monday at 8AM CT, four of the ten available dungeons are randomly assigned one of five weekly bonuses. 

## Overview

**Only four of the five bonuses are active each week**, so one of the bonuses is skipped each week. These four dungeons infused with tempting bonuses entices adventurers to seek out and slay monsters by the thousands. Once enough are slayed, the terrible dragon Dracothraxus will appear in one of the four dungeons for brave adventurers to battle to a fiery death.

!!! tip "Learning the active dungeons" Typing [dotw in game or !dotw in discord to learn the currently active dungeons

## DOTW Bonuses

### Boon of the Slayer

Higher chance creatures drop slayer weapons or instruments

### Gold Rush

Creatures drop more gold and loot this week

### Collector's Week

Creatures have a rare chance to drop statue and dye material

### Rift Surge

Rift shards, rift spawns, and rift gates have a higher chance to drop

### Scholar's Week

Skill gains are enhanced this week

## Dracothraxus

Once a certain number of enemies within the active dungeons are slain, the mighty dragon Dracothraxus will be summoned to one of the four dungeons. Adventurers must work together to defeat the terrible wyrm if they want to loot his massive hoard of gold.  

## Tips

- Try out different dungeons; you may be surprised which bonuses you like the most. 
- Branch out from your usual farm spots.
- Consider the bonus; weak mobs for Collector's Week vs strong mobs for Gold Rush.
- Learn how to fight Dracothraxus; he has several different fire attacks.

## Patch History

The DOTW system was introduced in **Patch 1.11**:

- **1.11:** Initial implementation — four dotw bonuses introduced
- **1.11.1:** Initial implementation — Adjust the amount of gold and loot that drops during the DotW Gold Rush bonus (with further adjustments now able to be made in-game); Champ spawn are excluded from DotW bonuses Created a gump for the [dotw command; Added a new dungeon bonus, Boon of the Slayer, which increases your chances for a slayer weapon or instrument; Fixed issue with dungeon milestones executing on non-active bonus dungeons
- **1.12:** Initial implementation — Dracothraxus has been modified to have a larger hit pool, a bit stronger, and a new special attack; Dracothraxus will now spawn in every dungeon; Fixed the issue with the Boon of the Slayer, mainly around slayer's not dropping as much as they should; Fixed the incorrect descriptions when entering a dungeon, cleaned up the general descriptions you see in Discord and in [dotw; Pets now see a boost in skill gains during Scholar's Week; Refactored how the DOTW system handles when a monster that was killed in a dungeon to prevent double processing; Lowered the chances to get rift shards during DOTW during normal mobs, however, increased the amount you get when killing a rift spawn (sorry, the rift shard gravy train is over)
