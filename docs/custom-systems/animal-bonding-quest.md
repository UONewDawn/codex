# Animal Bonding Quest

Discover rare pets around the world and complete your Sketchbook to gain an additional bond slot.

## Getting Started

The quest can be started by players with at least 80 Taming.

The quest giver can be found at the Moonglow Zoo located at these coordinates (4540, 1367).

Upon accepting the quest you will receive your blessed Sketchbook and Sketching Pencil.

## Sketchbook

Open the Sketchbook to see the list of all the pets you need to add.

To add a pet to your Sketchbook you first need to tame it, after that double click the Sketching Pencil and target the pet, then target the Sketchbook.

The pet will disappear and the Sketch will be added in your book alongside some information.

Pets can be traded among players.

Once you have filled the book, drag it on top of the quest giver to complete the quest.

## Required pets

These pets share the same spawn as the regular ones.

Every time a pet spawns, it has a chance of being a rare.

Since the spawn is shared, you will need to clear the area to force new pets to spawn.

Most Tamers use Tracking to help find these pets.
