# Battlegrounds

Battlegrounds are New Dawn's structured team PvP systems. These game modes pit two teams against each other in dedicated arenas with clear win conditions, automated queues, and stat tracking.

## Modes

### [Capture the Flag](capture-the-flag.md)

Two teams race to steal the enemy flag and return it to their base while defending their own. Matches take place on pirate-themed arenas in Felucca.

- 10v10 team battles
- Two unique arenas
- Demand-driven automated queue
- Earn **War Tokens** for participating and winning

[Learn more about Capture the Flag](capture-the-flag.md)

### [Conquest](conquest.md)

Teams fight to control multiple capture points across a contested map. The side holding more points bleeds the enemy's tickets — first team to zero loses.

- Objective-based territorial control
- Current arena: **Old Trinsic**
- Demand-driven automated queue
- Earn **War Tokens** for participating and winning

[Learn more about Conquest](conquest.md)

## Joining a Battleground

Both modes share the same signup flow. Head to **Dawn (Event Center)** and find the relevant signup book. Queues are demand-driven — signups open on a timer, fill to a minimum threshold, then a short fill window allows extra players before the match begins.

!!! note
    Multi-accounting is blocked across all battleground modes. If another character tied to your Discord account is already queued or in a match, you will be denied entry.

## War Tokens

Participating in and winning Battleground matches rewards **War Tokens**, which can be spent at the War Token vendor in Dawn.
