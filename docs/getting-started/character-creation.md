# Character Creation

Creating the perfect character in New Dawn requires understanding stats, skills, and templates. This guide will help you make informed decisions.

## Character Basics

### Stat Allocation

!!! tip "Stat Caps"
    Individual stat cap: 100
    Total stat cap: 225

### Skill Selection

You have **700 total skill points** that can be distributed across many skills:

- Skills range from 0.0 to 100.0
- Skills increase through use

## Customizing Your Appearance

### Character Appearance

When creating your character, you can customize:

- **Gender:** Male or Female
- **Hair Style:** Multiple options
- **Hair Color:** Various colors
- **Facial Hair:** For male characters
- **Skin Tone:** Multiple skin tones

### Starting Equipment

Based on your skill selection, you'll receive:

- Basic clothing
- A few starting weapons/tools
- Some gold coins
- Basic supplies

## Tips for Character Creation

!!! success "Best Practices"
    To help you get started:

    - **Start simple** - Choose a basic template for your first character
    - **Research skills** - Read our [Skills Guide](../game-mechanics/skills/index.md)
    - **Consider playstyle** - Pick skills that match how you want to play  
    - **Ask for advice** - The community can help on Discord

## Respeccing

!!! info "Changing Your Build"
    If you want to change your skills later:

    1. Skills can be lowered/raised naturally
    2. Use skill locks (up/down/locked)
    3. Some servers offer skill balls or respec items

    Check with staff about respec options!

## Multiple Characters

Consider creating multiple characters for different roles:

- **Main Character** - Your primary character
- **Crafter** - For creating items
- **Resource Gatherer** - Mining, lumberjacking
- **Merchant** - For trading and house vendors

## Next Steps

Once you've created your character:
