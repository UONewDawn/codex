# Britain

The capital city of Britannia and the seat of Lord British.

## Overview

Britain is the largest and most important city in Britannia. It serves as the political center and is often the most populated town on the server.

## Key Locations

### Castle British

- Home of Lord British
- Throne room and halls
- Healers available
- Beautiful architecture

### Britain Bank

- Main gathering spot
- Most populated bank
- Trading hub
- Social center

### West Britain

- Blacksmith shops
- Weaponsmith
- Armorer
- Training areas

### East Britain

- Mage shops
- Reagent vendors
- Tailors
- General provisions

## Vendors & Services

- **Blacksmith:** Weapons and armor repairs
- **Mage Shop:** Reagents and spellbooks
- **Provisioner:** General supplies
- **Tailor:** Clothing and leather
- **Stable:** Pet storage
- **Inn:** Rest and logout
- **Healer:** Free resurrection

## Nearby Areas

- Britain Graveyard (undead)
- Britain Farmlands (resources)
- Compassion Shrine
- Britain Sewers (dungeon entrance)

## Getting Here

- **Recall:** Bank or castle courtyard
- **Moongate:** Main moongate rotation
- **Walking:** Central location, accessible

## Why Choose Britain?

!!! success "Best for:"
    - New players (most help available)
    - Traders (highest traffic)
    - Social players (most populated)
    - All character types (has everything)
