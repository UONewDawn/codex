# Vesper

The industrial mining town of northern Britannia.

## Overview

Vesper is a thriving industrial town focused on mining, smithing, and crafting. It's popular among miners and blacksmiths.

## Key Locations

### Vesper Bank
- Crafter meeting spot
- Ore trading
- Tool exchanges

### Smithy District
- Multiple forges
- Blacksmith shops
- Crafting facilities

## Vendors & Services

- Blacksmith shops
- Mining supplies
- Tinker vendors
- General provisions

## Nearby Areas

- Minoc Mountains (mining)
- Northern forests
- Covetous dungeon entrance

## Why Choose Vesper?

- Excellent for miners
- Crafter community
- Near mountains
- Industrial atmosphere
