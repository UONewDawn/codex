# Moonglow

The city of magic, home to the Lycaeum and mage scholars.

## Overview

Moonglow is dedicated to the study of magic and knowledge. It houses the Lycaeum, a great library and center of learning.

## Key Locations

### The Lycaeum
- Massive library
- Mage trainers
- Knowledge and scrolls

### Moonglow Bank
- Mage gathering spot
- Spell trading
- Reagent discussions

## Vendors & Services

- Multiple mage shops
- Reagent vendors
- Inscription supplies
- Basic necessities

## Nearby Areas

- Perfect for mages
- Rich magic tradition
