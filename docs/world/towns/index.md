# Towns of Britannia

Discover the major towns and cities in the world of New Dawn.

## Major Towns

### Britain

The capital city of Britannia and center of commerce.

**Features:**
- Castle British
- Large bank (popular gathering spot)
- All vendor types
- Great starting location

[:octicons-arrow-right-24: Britain Guide](britain.md)

---

### Trinsic

The city of Honor and paladins.

**Features:**
- Paladin-themed city
- Good for honorable characters
- Complete vendor selection
- Southern location

[:octicons-arrow-right-24: Trinsic Guide](trinsic.md)

---

### Moonglow

The city of magic and scholars.

**Features:**
- Mage-focused town
- Lycaeum (library)
- Excellent for mages
- Island location

[:octicons-arrow-right-24: Moonglow Guide](moonglow.md)

---

### Vesper

The mining and industrial town.

**Features:**
- Mining community
- Crafters paradise
- Near mountains
- Good resource access

[:octicons-arrow-right-24: Vesper Guide](vesper.md)

## Other Notable Towns

### Yew

- Forest town
- Court of Justice
- Ranger/druid theme

### Skara Brae

- Spirituality shrine
- Island location
- Peaceful atmosphere

### Minoc

- Craftsman town
- Mining community
- Mountain access

### Jhelom

- Warriors and valor
- Combat-focused
- Dueling pits

## Town Features

### Banks

Every major town has:
- Secure storage
- Safe zones
- Social hubs

### Vendors

Find various shop types:
- Provisions
- Weapons/Armor
- Magic supplies
- Stables

### Facilities

Common amenities:
- Inns (logout)
- Taverns (food/drink)
- Smithies (forges/anvils)
- Libraries (knowledge)
