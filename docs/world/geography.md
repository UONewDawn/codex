# Geography

The diverse landscapes and regions of Britannia.

## Terrain Types

### Forests

Dense wooded areas throughout Britannia:

- **Resources:** Wood, animal hides
- **Creatures:** Bears, great harts, wolves
- **Notable:** Yew forest, Spiritwood

### Mountains

Rocky highlands rich in minerals:

- **Resources:** Ore, granite, gems
- **Creatures:** Harpies, ettins, orcs
- **Notable:** Minoc mountains, Serpent's Hold

### Swamps

Murky wetlands with unique dangers:

- **Resources:** Various reagents
- **Creatures:** Bog things, swamp dragons
- **Notable:** Trinsic swamps

### Deserts

Arid wastelands:

- **Resources:** Sand, special stones
- **Creatures:** Desert creatures
- **Notable:** Lost Lands (if available)

### Coastlines

Shores and beaches:

- **Resources:** Fishing spots
- **Creatures:** Sea serpents (in water)
- **Activities:** Fishing, travel

## Notable Regions

### Britain Territory

Central Britannia, most populated:
- Farmlands
- Training areas
- Easy hunting grounds

### Northern Wilderness

Rugged terrain near Vesper and Minoc:
- Mountain ranges
- Mining hotspots
- Harder creatures

### Southern Regions

Forests and swamps:
- Trinsic area
- Dense forests
- Tropical feeling

### Islands

Various island locations:
- Moonglow isle
- Magincia
- Ocllo
- Buccaneer's Den
