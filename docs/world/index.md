# The World of New Dawn

Explore the vast realm of Britannia and discover its many wonders.

## Overview

The world of New Dawn is rich with locations to explore, from bustling towns to dangerous dungeons and beautiful landscapes.

- Bank items
- Buy supplies
- Train skills
- Socialize
- Access vendors

## Map Overview

Britannia is divided into several continents and islands:

- **Main Continent:** Britain, Trinsic, Moonglow, Vesper
- **Lost Lands:** T2A content (if enabled)
- **Islands:** Various smaller locations

## Travel Methods

### Walking/Running

- Basic movement
- Explore at your own pace
- Find hidden areas

### Mounts

- Horses, llamas, etc.
- Faster travel
- Can be attacked while mounted

### Runes & Recall

- Mark locations with runes
- Recall to marked spots
- Gate travel for groups

### Moongates

- Fixed teleportation points
- Cycle through destinations
- Connect major towns

## Points of Interest

### Banks

Every major town has a bank:
- Store items safely
- Access from any bank
- Popular gathering spots

### Shops

Various vendor types:
- Mage shops (reagents)
- Weapon/armor shops
- Food and provisions
- Stable (pet storage)

### Training Areas

- Training dummies (combat)
- Picking chests (lockpicking)
- Safe monster spawns (newbies)
