# Stealing

Take items from other players and NPCs.

## Overview

Stealing allows you to take items from other players' backpacks and NPCs. A high-risk, high-reward skill that enables thief gameplay. Server rules vary significantly for thievery.

## Effects

### Item Theft

- Steal from player backpacks
- Take from NPC inventory
- Success based on skill and item weight
- Immediate criminal flag

### Weight Limits

Success based on:

- Your stealing skill
- Item weight
- Target's awareness
- Random chance

## Training

!!! warning
    These need refinement. Confirm your template in the Discord community.

### Early (0-30)

- Steal from NPCs in non-guard areas
- Practice on easy targets
- Light items first

### Intermediate (30-60)

- Steal heavier items
- Practice on various targets
- Improve success rate

### Advanced (60-85)

- Steal valuable items
- Better success rates
- Fewer failures

### Master (85-100)

- Expert thief
- Steal heavy items
- Maximum success rate

!!! warning "Criminal Activity"
    Stealing makes you criminal immediately. Guards will attack in town.

## Uses

### Player Theft

- Steal valuables from players
- Target specific items
- High risk, high reward
- Requires excellent execution

### NPC Theft

- Steal from merchant NPCs
- Lower risk
- Practice and profit
- Moderate rewards

## Criminal System

### Instant Criminal Flag

- Stealing makes you criminal
- Guards attack on sight
- Other players can attack freely
- Criminal timer must expire

### Murder Counts

- Stealing doesn't give murder counts
- But being killed as a thief has no penalty reduction
- Still considered criminal activity
