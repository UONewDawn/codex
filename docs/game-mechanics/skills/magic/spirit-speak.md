# Spirit Speak

## Overview

Spirit Speak passively enhances your Magery summons, making them last longer and harder to dispel.

Once you reach 100 and become a Grand Master, you will be able to see and communicate with ghosts.

Whe combined with Animal Lore, your summons will also be stronger.

## Summoning chance

Once you use Spirit Speak and establish contact with the netherworld, its effect will last around 3 minutes.

While under this effect, your 8th circle summoning spells will have an increased success rate.

At GM Spirit Speak, you have an 80% success rate.

## Increased healing

While under the effect of Spirit Speak, your healing restores additional health to your summoned creatures.

## Training

Train from Healer NPCs to reach around 30.

Spirit speak can be used on a 10 seconds cooldown, it doesn't have difficulty checks. Just use it until you reach 100.
