# Animal Lore

Identify and evaluate creatures and their abilities.

## Overview

Animal Lore allows you to examine creatures and pets, revealing detailed information about their stats, skills, and abilities. Essential for tamers and useful for all combat characters.

## Effects

### Creature Inspection
- View creature stats (STR, DEX, INT)
- See resistances and damage types
- Check loyalty and happiness (for pets)
- Identify special abilities

### Taming Synergy
Required for [Animal Taming](animal-taming.md):
- Evaluate tameable creatures
- Monitor pet development
- Assess pet potential

## Training

### Active Training
Use Animal Lore skill on:
- Wild animals
- Monsters
- Other players' pets
- Your own pets

### Passive Training
- Gains while taming creatures
- Improves alongside taming skill

!!! tip "Quick Training"
    Macro the skill on nearby animals. Each use provides a gain chance regardless of success.
