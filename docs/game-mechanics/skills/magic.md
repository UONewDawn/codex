# Magic Skills

Harness the arcane powers and become a master of magic in New Dawn.

## Core Magic Skills

### Magery

The primary spellcasting skill in Ultima Online.

**Spell Circles:**
- **First Circle (10-20 skill)** - Basic utility spells
- **Second Circle (20-30 skill)** - More utility, minor combat
- **Third Circle (30-40 skill)** - Teleport, combat spells
- **Fourth Circle (40-50 skill)** - Greater healing, recall
- **Fifth Circle (50-60 skill)** - Blade spirits, paralyze
- **Sixth Circle (60-70 skill)** - Invisibility, dispel field
- **Seventh Circle (70-80 skill)** - Gate travel, mass curse
- **Eighth Circle (80+ skill)** - Earthquake, resurrection

**Training:**
- 0-30: Cast low circle spells repeatedly
- 30-50: Teleport around, cast utility spells
- 50-70: Cast combat spells in dungeons
- 70-100: High circle spells, meditation between casts

**Reagents Required:**
- Black Pearl, Blood Moss, Garlic
- Ginseng, Mandrake Root, Nightshade
- Spider's Silk, Sulfurous Ash

**Pairs Well With:** Evaluating Intelligence, Meditation, Wrestling, Resisting Spells

---

### Evaluating Intelligence

Increases magic damage and spell effectiveness.

**Effects:**
- Boosts spell damage
- Better spell success rate
- Defensive benefit against spells

**Training:**
- Cast Evaluating Intelligence on creatures
- Passive gains during spell combat

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Essential for combat mages)

---

### Meditation

Regenerate mana faster.

**Effects:**
- Active mana regeneration
- Must be stationary
- Cannot be wearing metal armor
- Higher skill = faster regeneration

**Training:**
- Use skill when low on mana
- Gains when successfully meditating

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Essential for mages)

**Note:** Interrupted by movement and taking damage

---

### Resisting Spells

Defense against hostile magic.

**Effects:**
- Resist spell effects
- Reduce magic damage
- Prevent paralysis/poison
- Shorter duration on debuffs

**Training:**
- Get hit by hostile spells
- Have friend cast on you
- Fight enemy mages

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Essential for all templates)

## Support Magic Skills

### Inscription

Create spell scrolls and enhance spellbooks.

**Benefits:**
- Craft spell scrolls
- Increased spell damage (if wearing scribed spells)
- Create runebooks
- Slayer spellbooks

**Training:**
- Scribe low circle scrolls (0-30)
- Progress through circles
- Make runebooks at high skill

**Pairs Well With:** Magery, Alchemy

---

### Spirit Speak

Alternative mana regeneration (if Necromancy enabled).

**Effects:**
- Mana regeneration over time
- Works in armor
- Can be used while moving

**Training:**
- Use skill near corpses
- Passive gains during use

---

### Mysticism

Alternative magic school (if enabled).

**Effects:**
- Unique spell set
- Different from standard magery
- Special abilities

**Check with server:** May not be available on all shards

## Spell Categories

### Utility Spells

**Essential Utility:**
- **Recall** - Teleport to marked rune
- **Gate Travel** - Open portal for multiple people
- **Mark** - Mark location on rune
- **Teleport** - Short-range instant movement
- **Invisibility** - Become invisible

**Magic Lock/Unlock:**
- **Magic Lock** - Lock containers
- **Unlock Spell** - Open locked items

### Combat Spells

**Direct Damage:**
- **Lightning** - Fast, moderate damage
- **Energy Bolt** - Slower, high damage
- **Flamestrike** - Fire damage
- **Meteor Swarm** - Area damage
- **Chain Lightning** - Multiple targets

**Summoning:**
- **Blade Spirits** - Summon attacking spirits
- **Energy Vortex** - Powerful summon
- **Earth Elemental** - Summon elemental

**Debuffs:**
- **Curse** - Lower target's stats
- **Weaken** - Reduce strength
- **Clumsy** - Reduce dexterity
- **Feeblemind** - Reduce intelligence
- **Paralyze** - Freeze target

### Healing Spells

- **Heal** - Restore hit points
- **Greater Heal** - Restore more hit points
- **Cure** - Remove poison
- **Arch Cure** - Cure multiple targets
- **Resurrection** - Revive dead players

### Protection Spells

- **Magic Reflection** - Reflect spells
- **Protection** - Reduce spell damage
- **Reactive Armor** - Damage attackers
- **Bless** - Increase stats

### Field Spells

Create magical barriers:

- **Fire Field** - Damaging fire line
- **Poison Field** - Poison damage line
- **Paralyze Field** - Freeze enemies
- **Energy Field** - Impassable barrier
- **Wall of Stone** - Blocking wall

## Combat Strategies

### PvM (Player vs Monster)

**Basic Rotation:**
1. Open with Curse/Weaken
2. Cast Energy Bolt/Flamestrike
3. Follow up with Lightning
4. Heal when needed
5. Summon Blade Spirits for help

**Advanced:**
- Pre-cast spells (store spell)
- Kiting with teleport
- Field spell traps
- Summon management

### PvP (Player vs Player)

**Core Tactics:**
- Paralyze into Energy Bolt
- Magic Reflection for defense
- Poison spell into Explosion
- Curse before damage spells

!!! warning "PvP Maging"
    PvP maging has a high skill ceiling. Practice timing and spell combinations!

## Reagent Management

### Storage

- Organize by type in containers
- Use blessed reagent bags (if available)
- Keep extra stock at bank
- Buy in bulk for discounts

### Costs

Reagents are consumed on cast:

- Failed casts still consume reagents
- Higher circle = more reagents
- Keep well stocked for extended hunts

## Equipment

### Mage Armor

- Leather armor (allows meditation)
- Studded leather (if meditation works)
- Mage armor property (if available)
- Robes for aesthetics

### Spellbooks

- Standard spellbook (all spells)
- Slayer books (bonus vs creature type)
- Blessed books (don't lose on death)

### Accessories

- Mana regeneration items
- Lower reagent cost
- Lower mana cost
- Faster casting (if available)

## Training Tips

!!! tip "Fast Mage Training"
    - Macro low circle spells to 30
    - Progress through circles systematically
    - Cast in town (safe, near reagent vendors)
    - Use training dummies for Eval Int
    - Meditate between casts
    - Keep plenty of reagents
