# Crafting Skills

Master the art of creation and become a renowned craftsman in New Dawn.

## Overview

Crafting skills allow you to create equipment, tools, and consumables. These skills are essential for the economy and self-sufficiency.

## Primary Crafting Skills

### Blacksmithy

Forge metal weapons and armor.

**Creates:**
- Plate armor (chest, legs, arms, helm, gloves)
- Chainmail armor
- Metal shields
- Metal weapons (swords, maces, axes)

**Requirements:**
- Mining skill (to gather ore)
- Tinkering (to make tools)
- Forge and anvil

**Resources:**
- Iron ingots
- Colored ingots (if available)

**Training:**
- 0-30: Practice on ingots
- 30-50: Make daggers, chainmail
- 50-70: Make plate armor
- 70-100: Exceptional items, rare materials

**Pairs Well With:** Mining, Tinkering, Arms Lore

---

### Tailoring

Create cloth and leather armor and clothing.

**Creates:**
- Leather armor (full suits)
- Studded leather armor
- Clothing and robes
- Bandages

**Requirements:**
- Scissors or sewing kit
- Spinning wheel (for thread)
- Loom (for cloth)

**Resources:**
- Cloth/Leather
- Colored hides
- Thread

**Training:**
- 0-30: Bandages, cloth items
- 30-50: Leather armor
- 50-70: Studded leather
- 70-100: Exceptional items

**Pairs Well With:** Camping, Arms Lore

---

### Carpentry

Craft items from wood.

**Creates:**
- Furniture
- Wooden shields
- Staffs and clubs
- House addons

**Requirements:**
- Carpenter's tools
- Lumberjacking (to gather wood)

**Resources:**
- Logs and boards
- Different wood types

**Training:**
- 0-30: Kindling, shafts
- 30-50: Small furniture
- 50-70: Large furniture, shields
- 70-100: Advanced items

**Pairs Well With:** Lumberjacking, Tinkering

---

### Tinkering

Create tools, jewelry, and mechanical items.

**Creates:**
- Tools (all types)
- Jewelry (rings, bracelets)
- Keys and lockpicks
- Traps and golems

**Requirements:**
- Tinker tools
- Mining (for ingots)

**Resources:**
- Iron ingots
- Gems
- Parts

**Training:**
- 0-30: Lockpicks, keys
- 30-50: Tools, jewelry
- 50-70: Advanced items
- 70-100: Golems, complex items

**Pairs Well With:** Mining, Blacksmithy

---

### Alchemy

Brew potions and elixirs.

**Creates:**
- Healing potions (lesser to greater)
- Cure potions
- Explosion potions
- Stat potions
- Poison potions

**Requirements:**
- Mortar and pestle
- Bottles (empty)

**Resources:**
- Reagents
- Special ingredients

**Training:**
- 0-30: Lesser heal potions
- 30-50: Heal potions
- 50-70: Greater potions
- 70-100: Deadly poisons, kegs

**Pairs Well With:** Inscription, Magery

---

### Inscription

Scribe spells and create runebooks.

**Creates:**
- Spell scrolls (all circles)
- Runebooks
- Scrolls of power
- Slayer spellbooks

**Requirements:**
- Scribe's pen
- Blank scrolls

**Resources:**
- Blank scrolls
- Reagents

**Training:**
- 0-30: Low circle scrolls
- 30-50: Mid circle scrolls
- 50-70: High circle scrolls
- 70-100: Slayer books, runebooks

**Pairs Well With:** Magery, Alchemy

---

### Cooking

Prepare food and beverages.

**Creates:**
- Cooked meats
- Baked goods
- Special foods
- Beverages

**Requirements:**
- Skillet or flour mill
- Oven or fire source

**Resources:**
- Raw meat
- Flour, eggs
- Water, milk

**Training:**
- 0-30: Cook raw meat
- 30-60: Baked goods
- 60-100: Advanced recipes

**Pairs Well With:** Fishing, Camping

## Support Crafting Skills

### Arms Lore

Identify and assess equipment quality.

**Effects:**
- Determine weapon/armor quality
- Better repairs (reduces durability loss)
- Estimate damage ranges
- Identify exceptional items

**Training:**
- Use skill on weapons and armor
- Gains during identification

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Very Important for crafters)

---

### Item Identification

Identify magical properties.

**Effects:**
- Reveal magic item properties
- Determine item worth
- See charges on items

**Training:**
- Use on unidentified items
- Purchase items to identify

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Helpful for crafters/merchants)

## Resource Gathering

### Mining

Extract ore and gems from mountains.

**Yields:**
- Iron ore
- Colored ores (if available)
- Gems
- Granite

**Training:**
- 0-30: Iron ore anywhere
- 30-50: Mountain mining
- 50-70: Colored ore spots
- 70-100: Rare ore veins

**Requirements:** Pickaxe or shovel

---

### Lumberjacking

Harvest wood from trees.

**Yields:**
- Regular logs
- Colored wood (if available)

**Training:**
- 0-30: Any tree
- 30-50: Forest areas
- 50-70: Rare tree types
- 70-100: Special woods

**Requirements:** Axe or hatchet

**Bonus:** Increases damage with axes

## Crafting Mechanics

### Quality Levels

Items can be crafted at different qualities:

- **Regular** - Standard quality
- **Exceptional** - Higher quality, better stats
- **Masterwork** - Highest quality (if available)

!!! tip "Exceptional Items"
    Higher skill = better chance for exceptional quality
    Exceptional items have better stats and higher value

### Bulk Order Deeds (BODs)

Special crafting quests:

- NPCs give bulk orders
- Craft specific items
- Turn in for rewards
- Earn special resources

### Repair

Most crafters can repair their creations:

- Weapons lose durability
- Armor degrades
- Higher skill = better repairs
- Arms Lore reduces durability loss

## Tips for Crafters

!!! success "Crafting Success"
    - Set up a house for storage
    - Use ore carts and wood carts
    - Bulk craft to GM level
    - Organize resources in labeled containers
    - Keep tools in good supply
    - Join crafter guilds for help
    - Set up vendor to sell wares

## Economy & Trade

### Setting Prices

Consider:
- Material cost
- Time investment
- Exceptional quality bonus
- Market demand
- Competition

### Vendor Management

- Place vendors in high-traffic areas
- Competitive pricing
- Restock regularly
- Advertise your shop
