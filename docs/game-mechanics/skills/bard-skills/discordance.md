# Discordance

Weaken creatures and NPCs with discordant music.

## Overview

Discordance is a bard skill that allows you to weaken creatures and NPCs with discordant music, reducing their combat effectiveness. Part of the bard skill suite.

## Effects

### Creature Weakening

- Reduce creature stats and resistances
- Lower combat effectiveness
- Debuff enemy targets
- Duration based on skill

### Requirements

- Musical instrument equipped
- [Musicianship](musicianship.md) skill
- Line of sight to target

## Training

### Active Training

Use Discordance on:

- Aggressive monsters
- Town creatures
- Dungeon monsters
- Any targetable creature

### Training Tips

- Practice on low-level creatures first
- Train alongside Musicianship
- Use in safe combat scenarios
