# Swordsmanship

Use bladed weapons effectively in combat.

## Overview

Swordsmanship is a primary melee combat skill focused on wielding bladed weapons. This skill determines your accuracy and effectiveness when using swords, katanas, and pole arms.

## Weapons

### One-Handed Swords

- Longsword
- Broadsword
- Katana
- Viking Sword
- Cutlass
- Scimitar

### Two-Handed Weapons

- Halberd
- Bardiche

## Training

!!! warning
    These need refinement. Confirm your template in the Discord community.

### Early Training (0-30)

- Use training dummy in town
- Safe and consistent gains
- Cost: minimal weapon repair

### Intermediate (30-50)

- Hunt weak monsters (ettins, orcs)
- Switch to real combat
- Practice timing and movement

### Advanced (50-70)

- Fight mid-level monsters (trolls, ogres)
- Join group hunts
- Focus on tougher enemies

### Master Level (70-100)

- Battle strong monsters (dragons, demons)
- Champion spawns
- PvP practice

## Combat Tips

!!! tip "Effective Swordplay"
    - Keep weapon in good repair
    - Choose appropriate weapon for situation
    - Practice weapon special moves
    - Manage stamina carefully
    - Time healing between attacks

## Equipment Considerations

### Weapon Choice

- **Speed** - Faster weapons = more hits
- **Damage** - Higher base damage per hit
- **Requirements** - Check STR needed

### Armor

Balance protection with mobility:

- Heavy armor = better defense
- Light armor = faster movement
- Consider dexterity loss
