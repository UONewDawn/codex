# Forensic Evaluation

Investigate corpses and determine cause of death.

## Overview

Forensic Evaluation allows you to examine corpses and gather information about how they died, including who killed them. Useful for PvP situations and investigations.

## Effects

### Corpse Investigation
- Determine time of death
- Identify killer (player or monster)
- Cause of death information
- Examine player corpses

### Criminal Investigation
- Gather evidence
- Track murderers
- Investigation roleplay

## Training

### Active Training
Use Forensic Evaluation on:
- Player corpses
- Monster corpses
- Any dead body
- Repeatedly examine bodies

### Training Tips
- Practice in graveyards
- Examine after battles
- Macro on corpses

## Limitations

### Information Available
- Basic death information
- Killer identity (if applicable)
- Time of death
- Limited detail

### Skill Checks
- May fail at low skill

- Some information requires high skill
