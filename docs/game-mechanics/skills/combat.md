# Combat Skills

Master the art of warfare with these combat-focused skills in New Dawn.

## Weapon Skills

### Swordsmanship

Use bladed weapons effectively.

**Weapons:**
- Longsword, Broadsword, Katana
- Viking Sword, Cutlass, Scimitar
- Two-handed: Halberd, Bardiche

**Training:**
- 0-30: Training dummy
- 30-50: Weak monsters (ettins, orcs)
- 50-70: Mid-level monsters (trolls, ogres)
- 70-100: Strong monsters (dragons, demons)

**Pairs Well With:** Tactics, Anatomy, Healing, Parrying

---

### Fencing

Mastery of piercing weapons.

**Weapons:**
- Kryss, Dagger, War Fork
- Spear, Pitchfork
- Short Spear, Leafblade

**Training:**
Similar to Swordsmanship

**Pairs Well With:** Tactics, Anatomy, Healing, Poisoning

---

### Mace Fighting

Expertise with blunt weapons.

**Weapons:**
- Mace, War Mace, Maul
- War Hammer, Hammer Pick
- War Axe, Club

**Training:**
Similar to Swordsmanship

**Special:** Some maces have special effects against undead

**Pairs Well With:** Tactics, Anatomy, Healing, Parrying

---

### Archery

Ranged combat with bows and crossbows.

**Weapons:**
- Bow, Crossbow
- Heavy Crossbow
- Composite Bow (if available)

**Ammunition:**
- Arrows (for bows)
- Bolts (for crossbows)

**Training:**
- 0-30: Training dummy
- 30-50: Animals (bears, great harts)
- 50-70: Mid monsters at range
- 70-100: Difficult monsters, PvP

**Advantages:**
- Fight at range (safe)
- Kiting ability
- Poison arrows

**Pairs Well With:** Tactics, Anatomy, Hiding, Tracking

---

### Wrestling

Unarmed combat and defensive capability.

**Uses:**
- Disarm opponents
- Defensive ability for mages
- Stun punch

**Training:**
- 0-40: Training dummy
- 40-60: Sparring
- 60-100: Combat

**Special:** Often used by mages for defense without holding a weapon

**Pairs Well With:** Anatomy, Tactics, Healing

## Support Combat Skills

### Tactics

Increases damage output in combat.

**Effect:**
- Damage bonus to all physical attacks
- Scales with skill level
- Essential for all combat builds

**Training:**
- Gains during any combat
- Passive skill improvement

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Essential)

---

### Anatomy

Improves critical hit chance and healing.

**Effects:**
- Increased critical hit damage
- Better healing with bandages
- Damage bonus

**Training:**
- Gains during combat
- Passive improvement

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Essential)

---

### Parrying

Shield-based defense.

**Effects:**
- Block incoming attacks
- Reduces damage taken
- Active defense bonus

**Requirements:**
- Shield equipped
- Combat skill

**Training:**
- Take hits while holding shield
- Higher skill enemies = better gains

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Very Important)

---

### Healing

Use bandages to heal yourself and others.

**Effects:**
- Heal hit point damage
- Cure poison (with Anatomy)
- Resurrect players (with Anatomy)

**Training:**
- 0-30: Heal yourself when damaged
- 30-50: Heal during light combat
- 50-80: Heal during serious combat
- 80-100: Heal others, difficult wounds

**Requirements:**
- Bandages
- Anatomy skill (for best results)

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Essential)

---

### Resisting Spells

Defense against magic.

**Effects:**
- Resist hostile spells
- Reduce spell damage
- Avoid spell effects

**Training:**
- Get hit by spells
- Have someone cast on you
- Combat with mages

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Very Important for PvP)

## Special Abilities

### Weapon Special Moves

Many weapons have special attacks:

**Common Specials:**
- **Armor Ignore** - Bypass armor
- **Concussion Blow** - Reduce mana
- **Crushing Blow** - Extra damage
- **Disarm** - Remove opponent's weapon
- **Double Strike** - Hit twice
- **Paralyzing Blow** - Temporarily paralyze

!!! info "Using Specials"
    Special moves consume stamina or mana. Check your client's special moves book or icon.

## Combat Strategies

### Melee Combat

**Basic Strategy:**
1. Choose appropriate weapon
2. Keep healing supplies ready
3. Manage stamina
4. Use special moves wisely

**Advanced:**
- Weapon switching mid-combat
- Timing heal bandages
- Special move combos

### Ranged Combat

**Basic Strategy:**
1. Maintain distance from target
2. Kite dangerous enemies
3. Watch ammunition supply
4. Use terrain advantage

**Advanced:**
- Pre-poisoning arrows
- Target switching
- Hiding between shots

## Equipment Considerations

### Weapon Choice

Consider:
- **Damage type** - Some monsters have resistances
- **Speed** - Faster = more hits
- **Special move** - What ability it has
- **Requirements** - STR needed to use

### Armor

Balance:
- **Protection** - Damage reduction
- **Weight** - Movement penalty
- **Dexterity loss** - Affects swing speed
- **Material** - Different resistances

## PvP Combat Skills

For player vs player combat:

**Essential:**
- High weapon skill (100)
- Tactics (100)
- Anatomy (100)
- Healing (100)
- Resisting Spells (100)

**Recommended:**
- Parrying (80+)
- Magery for utility (40-80)

## Training Tips

!!! tip "Efficient Training"
    - Use training dummies to 30-40
    - Hunt creatures slightly above your level
    - Keep weapon in good repair
    - Train related skills together
    - Join group hunts for safety
