# Alchemy

Create powerful potions and enhance poisons.

## Overview

Alchemy allows you to craft a variety of potions including healing, poison, explosion, and buff potions. Higher skill levels enable stronger potions and better success rates.

## Effects

### Potion Crafting

- **Healing Potions** - Restore hit points
- **Poison Potions** - Used for weapon poisoning
- **Explosion Potions** - Deal damage in combat
- **Buff Potions** - Enhance stats temporarily

### Explosion Enhancement

- Your alchemy skill will also give a slight bonus to the min and max damage of an explosion potion.

## Uses

### PvM (Player vs Monster)

- Healing potions for survival
- Explosion potions for damage
- Buff potions for difficult encounters

### PvP (Player vs Player)

- Explosion potions for burst damage
- Cure potions for survival
