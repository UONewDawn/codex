# Alchemy

## Overview

Alchemy allows you to craft a variety of potions including healing, poison, explosion, and buff potions. Higher skill levels enable stronger potions and better success rates.

## Tools

In order to start crafting, you will need a mortar and pestle. You can purchase one from alchemist vendors.

## Codex binding

The Codex binding is used to bless runebooks.

|                                  Item                                  |             Resources              | Skill |
|:----------------------------------------------------------------------:|:----------------------------------:|:-----:|
| ![item-icon](../../../assets/items/codex-binding.png)<br>Codex Binding | 1 Arcane Ink<br>25 Codex Fragments |  80   |

### Arcane ink

Arcane ink can be purchased from the Codex Scholar at the Moonglow scribes (150,000 gp for the first purchase, with an increase of 50,000gp each purchase across all characters on that account).

### Codex fragments

Codex fragments can be obtained from rift encounters.

## Explosion potions bonus

Base damage against a player is between 5 min and 15 max. With GM Alchemy, it becomes 15 min and 20 max.

## Training

Alchemy is often trained along with Poisoning.

| Skill    | Potion          |
|----------|-----------------|
| 0 - 30   | Train from NPCs |
| 30 - 60  | Poison          |
| 60 - 97  | Greater Poison  |
| 97 - 100 | Deadly Poison   |
