# Blacksmithy

Craft and repair metal weapons and armor.

## Overview

Blacksmithy allows you to forge weapons, armor, and shields from metal ingots. One of the most valuable crafting skills, providing essential equipment for warriors and income through sales.

## Effects

### Weapon Crafting

Create metal weapons:

- Swords, axes, maces
- War hammers, war axes
- Polearms and halberds

### Armor Crafting

Forge metal armor:

- Plate armor
- Chainmail
- Ringmail
- Shields (all types)

### Repair

- Repair metal weapons and armor
- Higher skill = better repair success
- Can repair magical items

## Materials

### Tools

- **Smith's Hammer** - Required for all smithing

### Resources

- Iron ingots (basic)
- Colored ingots (shadow, copper, bronze, gold, agapite, verite, valorite)
- Access to anvil and forge
