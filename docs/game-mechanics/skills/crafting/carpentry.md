# Carpentry

Craft wooden furniture, instruments, and structures.

## Overview

Carpentry allows you to create a variety of wooden items including furniture, containers, instruments, and house addons. Essential for home decoration and some musical equipment.

## Effects

### Furniture Crafting

Create household items:

- Chairs, tables, benches
- Chests and containers
- Decorative items
- House addons

### Instrument Crafting

Make musical instruments:

- Lutes, drums, tambourines
- Harps and standing harps
- Required for bard skills

### Special Items

- Wooden shields
- Staves
- Training dummies
