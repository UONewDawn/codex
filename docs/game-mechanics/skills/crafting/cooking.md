# Cooking

Prepare food and create consumable items.

## Overview

Cooking allows you to prepare various foods from raw ingredients. While basic food satisfies hunger, higher-level cooking can create special foods with temporary benefits.

## Training

### Early (0-30)
- Buy flour from NPC baker
- Bake bread
- Cook raw meat

### Intermediate (30-60)
- Create more complex dishes
- Use various ingredients
- Experiment with recipes

### Advanced (60-85)
- Cook special dishes
- Create exceptional food
- Master recipes

### Master (85-100)
- Expert chef
- All recipes mastered
- Best quality food

## Materials

### Tools
- **Flour Mill** - For making flour
- **Oven** - For baking

### Ingredients
- Flour, eggs, milk
- Raw meat and fish
- Vegetables and fruits
- Special ingredients

## Related Skills

**Synergy With:**
- [Fishing](../resource-gathering/fishing.md) - Catch fish to cook
- [Animal Taming](../taming-and-animals/animal-taming.md) - Get milk and eggs

## Uses

### Personal Consumption
- Keep yourself fed
- Long adventuring supplies
- Emergency rations

### Sales
- Sell food to other players
- Supply taverns and inns (roleplay)

### Roleplay
- Chef and cook characters
- Tavern keeper
- Baker

## Importance

⭐⭐ (Useful utility, mostly roleplay)
