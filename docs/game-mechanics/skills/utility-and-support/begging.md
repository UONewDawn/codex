# Begging

Request gold from NPCs and players.

## Overview

Begging allows you to ask NPCs and players for gold. While not a combat or crafting skill, it can provide a small source of income and is useful for roleplay.

## Effects

### Gold Generation

- Request gold from NPCs
- Success based on skill level
- Limited gold per NPC per day
- Can target players (they must consent)

### Success Rate

Higher skill = more frequent success:

- 0-30: Very rare success
- 30-60: Occasional gold
- 60-100: Better rates and amounts

## Training

### Active Training

Use Begging on:

- Town NPCs
- Shopkeepers
- Guards
- Other friendly NPCs

### Training Tips

- Visit multiple towns
- Target different NPCs
- Be patient with gains
