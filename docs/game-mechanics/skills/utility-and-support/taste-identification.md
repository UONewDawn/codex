# Taste Identification

Identify poisoned food and detect poison.

## Overview

Taste Identification allows you to detect poison in food and beverages before consuming them. Also provides some resistance to poison attempts. Situational skill with limited applications.

## Effects

### Poison Detection

- Detect poisoned food
- Identify poison in drinks
- Check food safety
- Prevent poisoning

## Training

### Active Training

Use Taste Identification on:

- Food items
- Potions
- Any consumable
- Repeated attempts

### Passive Training

- May gain when poisoned
- Slow passive gains
- Active training faster
