# Taste Identification

## Overview

Taste Identification allows you to identify the level of a potion and detect poison in food.

You can also identify the quality of Honey and the type of Beeswax.

## Training

Taste Identification can be used on a 1 second cooldown, it doesn't have any difficulty checks.

Repeatedly Taste ID the same potion until reaching 100.
