# Arms Lore

Evaluate weapons and armor, identify quality and durability.

## Overview

Arms Lore allows you to assess weapons and armor, revealing their condition, durability, and special properties. Useful for smiths, traders, and anyone who wants to evaluate gear quality.

## Effects

### Item Evaluation

- Check weapon/armor durability
- Identify item quality and crafting materials
- Assess damage ranges on weapons
- View armor protection values

## Training

### Active Training

Use Arms Lore on:

- Your own equipment
- Other players' gear
- Weapons in shops
- Armor pieces

### Passive Training

- Gains while repairing items (with Blacksmithy)
- Improves during crafting sessions

!!! tip "Fast Training"
    Macro Arms Lore on a piece of gear. Each attempt gives gain chance.

## Uses

### Trading

- Evaluate item value before purchase
- Assess durability before buying used gear

### Maintenance

- Check equipment condition
- Plan repairs before items break
- Monitor gear degradation
