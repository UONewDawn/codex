# Anatomy

Improves critical hit chance, damage, and healing effectiveness.

## Overview

Anatomy is a versatile support skill that enhances both offensive combat and healing. It's essential for all physical combat builds and significantly improves bandage healing.

## Effects

### Combat Benefits
- **Increased Critical Hit Chance** - More frequent critical strikes
- **Bonus Damage** - Additional damage on all attacks
- **Better DPS** - Combines with Tactics for maximum output

### Healing Benefits
- **Improved Bandage Healing** - Heal more HP per bandage
- **Cure Poison** - Can cure poison with bandages (requires skill)
- **Resurrect** - Revive dead players with bandages (high skill)

## Training

### Passive Gains
Anatomy improves during combat:
- Gains during any physical combat
- Trains alongside weapon skills
- No special actions needed

### Active Training
- Use Anatomy skill on creatures
- Examine corpses
- Practice during hunts

!!! tip "Natural Progression"
    Anatomy trains naturally as you fight. Just keep it locked up and it will reach 100 through normal combat.

## Healing Synergy

### With Healing Skill
Anatomy + Healing creates powerful synergy:
- Base heal from Healing skill
- + Anatomy bonus
- + Dexterity modifier
- = Maximum HP restored

### Poison Curing
Requirements for curing poison with bandages:
- Healing skill: 60+
- Anatomy skill: 60+
- Higher skills = better success rate

### Resurrection
Requirements for resurrection with bandages:
- Healing skill: 80+
- Anatomy skill: 80+
- Must be next to corpse

## Combat Impact

### Damage Calculation
Your total damage includes:
- Base weapon damage
- Strength bonus
- Tactics bonus
- **Anatomy bonus** ⭐
- Critical hit multiplier

### Critical Hits
Higher Anatomy = more critical hits:
- Extra damage on successful critical
- Visual "critical hit" message
- Significant DPS increase
