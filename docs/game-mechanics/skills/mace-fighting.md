# Mace Fighting

Expertise with blunt weapons and crushing force.

## Overview

Mace Fighting is the skill for wielding blunt weapons. Some maces have special bonuses against specific enemy types, particularly undead creatures.

## Weapons

### One-Handed Maces
- Mace
- War Mace
- Maul
- Club

### Heavy Weapons
- War Hammer
- Hammer Pick

### Axes (Blunt Category)
- War Axe

## Training

### Early Training (0-30)

- Training dummy in town
- Consistent skill gains
- Practice swings

### Intermediate (30-50)
- Weak monsters (ettins, orcs)
- Real combat experience
- Learn enemy patterns

### Advanced (50-70)
- Mid-level monsters (trolls, ogres)
- Group hunts
- Build combat confidence

### Master Level (70-100)
- Strong monsters (dragons, demons)
- Undead hunting with bonuses
- PvP mastery

## Special Advantages

### Undead Bonus
Some maces deal extra damage to undead:
- Liches
- Zombies
- Skeletons
- Bone demons

!!! tip "Undead Hunting"
    Bring a mace to graveyards and undead dungeons for significant damage bonuses!

### Crushing Power
Blunt weapons excel at:
- Breaking through armor
- Stunning attacks
- Area damage effects

## Weapon Special Moves

Mace special abilities:
- **Crushing Blow** - Massive damage spike
- **Concussion Blow** - Drain target's mana
- **Disarm** - Remove opponent's weapon
- **Whirlwind Attack** - AoE damage
