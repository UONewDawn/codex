# Blacksmithy

Craft and repair metal weapons and armor.

## Overview

Blacksmithy allows you to forge weapons, armor, and shields from metal ingots. One of the most valuable crafting skills, providing essential equipment for warriors and income through sales.

## Effects

### Weapon Crafting
Create metal weapons:
- Swords, axes, maces
- War hammers, war axes
- Polearms and halberds
- Exceptional quality for bonuses

### Armor Crafting
Forge metal armor:
- Plate armor (best physical defense)
- Chainmail
- Ringmail
- Shields (all types)

### Repair
- Repair metal weapons and armor
- Higher skill = better repair success
- Can repair magical items

## Training

### Early (0-30)
- Buy iron ingots from NPC
- Craft daggers and ringmail gloves
- Smith near anvil and forge

### Intermediate (30-60)
- Mine your own iron
- Craft chainmail and weapons
- Smelt ingots from ore

### Advanced (60-85)
- Craft plate armor
- Make high-quality weapons
- Use colored ingots for better items

### Master (85-100)
- Craft exceptional items regularly
- Use valorite and verite ingots
- Create top-tier equipment

## Materials

### Tools
- **Smith's Hammer** - Required for all smithing
- **Tongs** - Sometimes required

### Resources
- Iron ingots (basic)
- Colored ingots (shadow, copper, bronze, gold, agapite, verite, valorite)
- Access to anvil and forge

## Crafting Quality

### Normal Items
- Basic stats
- Standard durability

### Exceptional Items
- Bonus to damage/defense
- Higher durability
- Better resale value
- Requires high skill

## Metal Types

### Common Metals
- **Iron** - Most common, basic quality
- **Shadow** - Slightly better
- **Copper** - Moderate enhancement

### Rare Metals
- **Bronze** - Good quality
- **Gold** - High quality
- **Agapite** - Excellent
- **Verite** - Superior
- **Valorite** - Best possible

## Economic Value

### High Demand Items
- Plate armor sets
- Quality weapons
- Shields
- Repair services

### Profit Potential
- Sell exceptional items
- Repair services for players
- Bulk orders from NPCs
- Custom orders
