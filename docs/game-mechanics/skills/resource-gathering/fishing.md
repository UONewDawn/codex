# Fishing

Catch fish and treasure from water.

## Overview

Fishing allows you to catch fish from any body of water. Beyond basic fish for food, skilled fishermen can catch rare fish, treasure, and special items.

## Effects

### Fish Catching

- Catch fish for cooking
- Various fish types
- Success rate based on skill
- Automatic weight calculation

### Special Catches

At higher skills:

- Treasure chests
- Special fish
- Magic items
- Rare finds

## Training

### Early (0-30)

- Buy fishing pole from NPC
- Fish from any water
- Catch common fish

### Intermediate (30-60)

- Fish from boats
- Catch better fish
- Occasional special items

### Advanced (60-85)

- Fish for treasure
- Rare catches increase
- Master fishing spots

### Master (85-100)

- Expert fisherman
- Best treasure chances
- Legendary catches

## Materials

### Tools

- **Fishing Pole** - Required for fishing
- Boat (optional, for deeper water)

## Related Skills

**Synergy With:**

- [Cooking](../crafting/cooking.md) - Prepare your catches

## Uses

### Food Source

- Catch fish for cooking
- Self-sufficient food supply
- Sell fish to cooks

### Treasure Hunting

- Find treasure chests
- Discover magic items
- Special fishing treasures

### Relaxation

- Peaceful activity
- Roleplay opportunities
- Social fishing trips

## Fishing Locations

### Shore Fishing

- Any body of water
- Ponds, rivers, ocean shore
- Easy access

### Deep Water Fishing

- Requires boat
- Better catches
- More treasure opportunities

## Economic Value

### Income Sources

- Treasure chest contents
- Rare fish sales
- Special item finds

## Importance

⭐⭐⭐ (Good supplemental skill, relaxing activity)
