# Combat Skills

Master the art of warfare with these combat-focused skills in New Dawn.

## Weapon Skills

### Swordsmanship

Use bladed weapons effectively.

**Weapons:**

- Longsword, Broadsword, Katana
- Viking Sword, Cutlass, Scimitar

**Training:**

- 0-30: Training dummy
- 30-50: Weak monsters (ettins, orcs)
- 50-70: Mid-level monsters (trolls, ogres)
- 70-100: Strong monsters (dragons, demons)

**Pairs Well With:** Tactics, Anatomy, Healing, Parrying

---

### Fencing

Mastery of piercing weapons.

**Weapons:**

- Kryss, Dagger, War Fork
- Spear, Pitchfork
- Short Spear

**Training:**
Similar to Swordsmanship

**Pairs Well With:** Tactics, Anatomy, Healing, Poisoning

---

### Mace Fighting

Expertise with blunt weapons.

**Weapons:**

- Mace, War Mace, Maul
- War Hammer, Hammer Pick
- War Axe, Club

**Training:**
Similar to Swordsmanship

**Pairs Well With:** Tactics, Anatomy, Healing, Parrying

---

### Archery

Ranged combat with bows and crossbows.

**Weapons:**

- Bow, Crossbow
- Heavy Crossbow
- Composite Bow (if available)

**Ammunition:**

- Arrows (for bows)
- Bolts (for crossbows)

**Training:**

- 0-30: Training dummy
- 30-50: Animals (bears, great harts)
- 50-70: Mid monsters at range
- 70-100: Difficult monsters, PvP

**Advantages:**

- Fight at range (safe)
- Kiting ability

**Pairs Well With:** Tactics, Anatomy, Hiding, Tracking

---

### Wrestling

Unarmed combat and defensive capability.

**Uses:**

- Disarm opponents
- Defensive ability for mages
- Stun punch

**Training:**

- 0-40: Training dummy
- 40-60: Sparring
- 60-100: Combat

**Special:** Often used by mages for defense without holding a weapon

**Pairs Well With:** Anatomy, Tactics, Healing

## Support Combat Skills

### Tactics

Increases damage output in combat.

**Effect:**

- Damage bonus to all physical attacks
- Formula: +0.625% damage per skill point (max +6.25% at 100)
- In pre-AOS: Additional modifier at 50+ skill
- Essential for all combat builds

**Training:**

- Gains passively during any combat
- Checked automatically on every hit

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Essential)

---

### Anatomy

Improves damage output and healing effectiveness.

**Effects:**

- Damage bonus: +0.5% per skill point (max +5% at 100)
- Better healing with bandages (increases heal amount)
- Required for curing poison (60+ Anatomy, 60+ Healing)
- Required for resurrection (80+ Anatomy, 80+ Healing)

**Training:**

- Gains passively during combat
- Checked automatically on every hit

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Essential)

---

### Parrying

Defensive skill for blocking attacks.

**Effects:**

- Block incoming melee attacks
- Much more effective with a shield equipped
- Can parry with weapons (much lower chance)
- Shield parry: Up to 30% block chance at GM
- Weapon parry: Very low chance

**Requirements:**

- Shield (recommended) or weapon equipped
- Cannot parry with fists or ranged weapons

**Training:**

- Gains when successfully blocking attacks
- Being attacked while holding shield
- Higher skill enemies = better gains

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Very Important)

---

### Healing

Use bandages to heal yourself and others.

**Effects:**

- Heal hit point damage
- Cure poison (requires 60+ Healing AND 60+ Anatomy)
- Resurrect players (requires 80+ Healing AND 80+ Anatomy)
- Heal amount scales with Healing and Anatomy

**Training:**

- 0-30: Heal yourself when damaged
- 30-50: Heal during light combat
- 50-80: Heal during serious combat
- 80-100: Heal others, difficult wounds

**Requirements:**

- Bandages in backpack
- Anatomy skill strongly recommended

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Essential)

---

### Resisting Spells

Defense against magic.

**Effects:**

- Resist hostile spells
- Reduce spell damage
- Avoid spell effects

**Training:**

- Get hit by spells
- Have someone cast on you
- Combat with mages

**Importance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Very Important for PvP)

## Special Abilities

### Weapon Special Moves

Many weapons have special attacks:

**Common Specials:**

- **Armor Ignore** - Bypass physical resistance (90% damage)
- **Bleed Attack** - Cause bleeding damage over time
- **Concussion Blow** - Reduce target's mana
- **Crushing Blow** - Extra damage
- **Disarm** - Remove opponent's weapon
- **Dismount** - Knock opponent off mount
- **Double Strike** - Strike twice quickly
- **Infectious Strike** - Reduce healing effectiveness
- **Mortal Strike** - Prevent healing temporarily
- **Paralyzing Blow** - Freeze target (3-6 seconds)
- **Shadow Strike** - Damage ignores armor
- **Whirlwind Attack** - Hit all nearby enemies

!!! info "Using Specials"
    Special moves consume mana. Primary weapon abilities require 70 skill, secondary abilities require 90 skill and 90 Tactics.

## Combat Strategies

### Melee Combat

**Basic Strategy:**
1. Choose appropriate weapon
2. Keep healing supplies ready
3. Manage stamina
4. Use special moves wisely

**Advanced:**

- Weapon switching mid-combat
- Timing heal bandages
- Special move combos

### Ranged Combat

**Basic Strategy:**
1. Maintain distance from target
2. Kite dangerous enemies
3. Watch ammunition supply
4. Use terrain advantage

**Advanced:**

- Target switching
- Hiding between shots
- Kiting techniques

## Equipment Considerations

### Weapon Choice

Consider:
- **Damage type** - Some monsters have resistances
- **Speed** - Faster = more hits
- **Special move** - What ability it has
- **Requirements** - STR needed to use

### Armor

Balance:
- **Protection** - Damage reduction
- **Weight** - Movement penalty
- **Dexterity loss** - Affects swing speed
- **Material** - Different resistances

## PvP Combat Skills

For player vs player combat:

**Essential:**

- High weapon skill (100)
- Tactics (100)
- Anatomy (100)
- Healing (100)
- Resisting Spells (100)

**Recommended:**

- Parrying (80+)
- Magery for utility (40-80)

## Training Tips

!!! tip "Efficient Training"
    - Use training dummies to 30-40
    - Hunt creatures slightly above your level
    - Keep weapon in good repair
    - Train related skills together
    - Join group hunts for safety
