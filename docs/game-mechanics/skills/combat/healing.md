# Healing

## Overview

Healing must be paired with Anatomy to be effective, you can heal wounds, poison and even resurrect others with the use of bandages.

## Requirements

A minimum of 60 in both Healing and Anatomy will give you a 80% chance to cure poison.

A minimum of 80 in both Healing and Anatomy will give you a 25% chance of resurrecting others.

## Healing power

Anatomy directly affects the amount you can heal.

`Minimum amount = (Anatomy รท 5) + (Healing รท 5) + 3`

`Maximum amount = (Anatomy รท 5) + (Healing รท 2) + 10`

With both Healing and Anatomy at 100 for example, you could heal between 43 and 80 hit points.

## Training

You will need to stockpile a large amount of cloth for your bandages.

Healing or curing others is faster than healing yourself, to be more efficient consider training two characters combat skill while healing each other.

| Skill    | Bandage                                                                                                    |
|----------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 0 - 30   | Train from NPCs                                                                                            |
| 30 - 60  | Heal damage                                                                                                |
| 60 - 80  | Cure poison                                                                                                |
| 80 - 100 | Resurrect :material-information-outline:{ title="Resurrecting is slower than curing someone else poison" } |
