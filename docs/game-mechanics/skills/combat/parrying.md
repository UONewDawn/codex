# Parrying

Shield-based defense and damage reduction.

## Overview

Parrying allows you to block incoming attacks with a shield. This defensive skill significantly reduces damage taken and is essential for tank builds.

## Training

!!! warning
    These need refinement. Confirm your template in the Discord community.

### Combat Training

Parrying improves by taking hits:

- Equip a shield
- Engage in combat
- Get hit by enemies
- Higher skill enemies = better gains

### Training Methods

!!! warning
    These need refinement. Confirm your template in the Discord community.

**Early (0-40):**

- Fight weak monsters while blocking
- Training arena sparring
- Safe controlled environment

**Intermediate (40-70):**

- Hunt mid-level monsters
- Take more hits intentionally
- Group hunting (let them hit you)

**Advanced (70-100):**

- Fight strong monsters
- Champion spawns
- PvP practice

!!! warning "Training Caution"
    You must be hit to train Parrying. Bring healing supplies and don't train on enemies that hit too hard!

## Shield Types

### Light Shields

- Wooden Shield
- Buckler
- Lower defense, lighter weight

### Medium Shields

- Metal Shield
- Heater Shield
- Balanced protection

### Heavy Shields

- Tower Shield
- Chaos Shield
- Maximum protection, higher requirements

!!! tip "Shield Selection"
    Choose shield based on your strength and armor weight. Heavier shields provide better protection but have higher requirements.
