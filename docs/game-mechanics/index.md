## The Skill System

New Dawn uses a skill-based progression system where:

- Characters have 700 total skill points
- Skills range from 0.0 to 100.0
- Skills improve through use
- No class restrictions

## Character Stats

Three primary statistics define your character:

| Stat | Primary Effect | Secondary Effects |
|------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Strength** | Hit Points, Melee Damage | Carrying Capacity |
| **Dexterity** | Stamina, Swing Speed | Defense Chance |
| **Intelligence** | Mana Pool | Magic Effectiveness |

## Combat Fundamentals

Combat in UO is based on several factors:

- Weapon skill vs. opponent's defense
- Tactics skill for damage bonus
- Anatomy for critical hits
- Equipment quality and properties

## Magic & Spellcasting

Multiple magic systems exist:

- **Magery** - Traditional spell casting
- **Necromancy** - Death magic (if enabled)
- **Chivalry** - Paladin spells (if enabled)
- **Bushido/Ninjitsu** - Eastern arts (if enabled)

## Crafting & Economy

Create your own equipment and sell to others:

- Blacksmithy for metal weapons/armor
- Tailoring for cloth and leather
- Carpentry for furniture and tools
- Alchemy for potions
- And many more!

[:octicons-arrow-right-24: Crafting Guide](crafting.md)

## Resource Gathering

Before crafting, you'll need resources:

- **Mining** - Ore and gems
- **Lumberjacking** - Wood
- **Fishing** - Fish and treasure
- **Skinning** - Leather and hides

## Character Development

### Skill Progression

Skills increase through use:

1. **0-50:** Fast gains, basic activities
2. **50-80:** Moderate gains, advanced activities
3. **80-100:** Slow gains, expert-level content

### Stat Growth

Stats increase over time:

- Gain points by using related skills
- Maximum 225 total stat points
- Individual stat cap: 100

## Game Balance

New Dawn maintains balance through:

- Skill caps limiting specialization
- Stat caps requiring trade-offs
- Equipment durability
- Resource scarcity
- PvP systems (if applicable)

## Quick Reference
